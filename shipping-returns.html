Shipping Coverage

We proudly offer India-wide shipping. Whether you are located in a major metropolitan city or a remote tier-3 town, we partner with reliable logistics providers to ensure your order reaches you safely. Specific serviceability for extremely remote PIN codes will be confirmed during the inquiry or checkout process.

Delivery Timelines

Delivery schedules vary based on the nature of the product and your location:

  • Standard Items: Dispatched within 24-48 hours. Delivered in 3-7 business days.
  • Custom/Made-to-Order Items: Production takes 1-3 weeks depending on complexity, followed by standard shipping times.
  • Remote Locations: May require an additional 3-5 business days for final delivery.

Shipping Charges

Shipping costs are calculated based on the volumetric weight of your order and the destination PIN code. Exact shipping charges will be transparently quoted to you during your initial inquiry or at the checkout stage before any payment is processed. We occasionally run free-shipping promotions, which will be applied automatically if your order qualifies.

Order Tracking

We believe in keeping you updated every step of the way. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive real-time tracking updates directly via WhatsApp and Email. You will be provided with a tracking link and the courier partner's details to monitor your shipment's journey.

Return Eligibility

We maintain a flexible and customer-friendly return policy. To be eligible for a return:

  • The return request must be raised within 7 days of delivery.
  • The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • It must be in the original packaging with all tags, manuals, and accessories intact.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned due to hygiene, customization, or clearance reasons. These include:

  • Custom-made or personalized orders.
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance".
  • Gift cards and downloadable software products.
  • Items that show obvious signs of use, wear, or damage caused post-delivery.

Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives damaged, please do not accept the package if the outer box is severely compromised. If you discover damage after opening:

Required Action:

  1. Take clear photos of the damaged item AND the packaging.
  2. Provide an unboxing video if possible (highly recommended).
  3. Contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery.

We will arrange an expedited replacement or full refund upon verification of the damage.

Refund Process & Timeline

Once your returned item is received and inspected at our facility (usually within 48 hours of receipt), we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, the refund will be processed to your original method of payment. Please allow 5-7 business days for the funds to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or credit card issuer.

Warranty vs. Return

It's important to distinguish between returns and warranty claims:

Returns

Applicable for brand-new, unused items you no longer want. Must be initiated within 7 days.

Warranty

Applicable for items that develop a manufacturing defect over time. Covers repair or replacement depending on the product's specific warranty period.
