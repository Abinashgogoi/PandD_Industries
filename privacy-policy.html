Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data to ensure a secure and transparent experience.

Last updated: March 3, 2026

1. Overview

At P&D Industries, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of the data you provide to us through our website and related communication channels.

By interacting with our services, requesting quotes, or communicating with us, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with our industrial products and services. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address (e.g., when emailing mainadigitalstore@gmail.com), and phone number.
  • Business Details: Company name, operational requirements, and project specifications necessary for quoting.
  • Communication Data: Inquiry messages, WhatsApp chat history, and emails sent to our support or sales team.

3. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is strictly used to facilitate business operations and improve your customer experience:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide accurate product quotes.
  • To process orders, arrange shipping, and handle returns.
  • To provide ongoing customer support and technical assistance.
  • To send important updates regarding your orders or changes to our policies.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to outside marketing firms.

4. Third-Party Services & WhatsApp

To provide rapid and convenient customer service, we utilize third-party communication platforms, primarily WhatsApp (+91 87248 28479).

Please note that any information shared via WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policies and end-to-end encryption standards. While we maintain strict confidentiality on our end, we advise against sending highly sensitive financial information through chat applications.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website utilizes minimal tracking. We do not use intrusive third-party advertising cookies. Any cookies used are strictly for essential website functionality (such as keeping your session active) and basic, anonymized performance analytics to ensure our site runs smoothly across devices.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to your data is restricted solely to authorized P&D Industries personnel who require it to perform their job duties.

7. Data Retention & User Rights

Retention: We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Rights: You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data (subject to legal or operational constraints).

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Questions about privacy?

If you have any concerns about how your data is handled, our dedicated support team is available to assist you.
